Grid Smasher

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Grid Smasher ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Grid Smasher iOS app.

Information We Collect

  • Account and identifiers: anonymous Firebase user ID, Game Center player ID, and Game Center display name (if available).
  • Gameplay and leaderboard data: scores, lines smashed, session duration, achievement progress, and ranking data you submit.
  • Notification data: push notification token(s) and notification permission status.
  • Analytics data: in-app events such as game starts/ends, share actions, ad interactions, and feature usage.
  • Advertising data: ad performance and device/ad identifiers used by our ad provider. If you allow tracking through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, ads may be more personalized.
  • Purchase data: in-app purchase entitlement status (for Remove Ads). Payment processing is handled by Apple, and we do not receive your full payment card details.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the game and its features.
  • Maintain leaderboards, achievements, and player progression.
  • Send optional push notifications if you grant permission.
  • Serve and measure ads, including ad frequency controls and optional personalization.
  • Process and restore in-app purchases.
  • Monitor app performance, improve gameplay, and prevent abuse.

How Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal information. We share data with service providers only as needed to run the app, including:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, analytics, cloud functions, database, messaging/notifications, remote config).
  • Google AdMob (ads and related measurement).
  • Apple services such as Game Center and StoreKit (sign-in, achievements, and purchases).

Data Retention

  • Daily leaderboard entries are retained for a limited period (currently up to 8 days) for leaderboard operation.
  • Achievement/profile metadata and push token data are retained while needed to provide app features, unless deleted earlier.
  • Some analytics and ad data are retained according to third-party provider retention settings and policies.

Your Choices

  • Notifications: you can enable or disable notifications in iOS Settings.
  • Tracking: you can allow or deny tracking, and change this anytime in iOS Settings.
  • Ads: you can purchase Remove Ads to disable interstitial ads in the app.
  • Data requests: contact us to request access or deletion where applicable.

Children's Privacy

Grid Smasher is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us and we will work to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date on this page when changes are made.
